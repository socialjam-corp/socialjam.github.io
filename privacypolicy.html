Who We Are

Data Controller: SocialJam S.L., a company incorporated in Spain, is the data controller responsible for your personal data when you use the Skeep app.

Skeep is a mobile-first social gaming application that lets users discover and play turn-based games, challenge friends, and communicate via messaging and group chats. Given the social and interactive nature of our Service, we collect and process certain personal data to provide and improve these features.

Our registered address is: SocialJam S.L., Carrer de Ramón Llull 2, 1r 2a, 08923 Santa Coloma de Gramenet, Spain. You can reach our privacy team at hi@skeep.me.

03

How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect for the following purposes:

  • Providing the Service — creating and managing your account, enabling gameplay, matchmaking, messaging, and group chats
  • Personalisation — tailoring your game feed, recommending challenges, and displaying relevant content based on your activity
  • Safety & moderation — reviewing reported content, investigating abuse, enforcing our Terms & Conditions, and protecting users from harm
  • Security & fraud prevention — detecting and preventing cheating, account takeovers, and other malicious activity
  • Communications — sending service-related notifications (security alerts, policy updates) and, where you have opted in, promotional messages
  • Analytics & improvement — understanding how users interact with the app to fix bugs, improve features, and develop new ones
  • Advertising — serving ads to free-tier users, and measuring ad performance (see Section 6)
  • Legal compliance — meeting our obligations under applicable law, including responding to lawful requests from authorities

Messages & Chat Data

Messages are stored to deliver them reliably and to enable our safety team to review content when a report is filed. We do not routinely read private messages. Automated systems may scan messages to detect spam, illegal content, or policy violations. Except as required by law or described in this policy, message content is not shared with third parties.

Sharing Your Data

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it only in the following circumstances:

With Other Users

Your username, display name, profile picture, and game stats are visible to other Skeep users. Messages you send are visible to the recipient(s) of those messages. Your friends list may be visible depending on your privacy settings.

With Service Providers

We work with trusted third-party companies to help operate the Service, including cloud hosting providers, analytics platforms, customer support tools, payment processors, and moderation services. These providers process data only on our behalf and under strict data processing agreements.

With Advertising Partners

For free-tier users, we share limited data (such as device identifiers and usage signals) with advertising networks to serve and measure ads. See Section 6 for more detail and opt-out options.

For Legal Reasons

We may disclose your data if required to do so by law, regulation, or valid legal process; to protect the rights, safety, or property of SocialJam S.L., our users, or the public; or in connection with a corporate transaction such as a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, in which case the successor entity will be bound by this Privacy Policy.

Important

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for their own marketing purposes.

06

Advertising & Tracking

Free-tier users see ads within the Skeep app. We use third-party advertising networks that may use cookies, device identifiers, and similar technologies to serve ads that are relevant to you based on your interests and activity.

What Data Is Used for Ads

  • Device advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android), where you have granted permission
  • General location derived from IP address
  • In-app activity and usage patterns
  • Device type and operating system

Your Choices

  • iOS: Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking and disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
  • Android: Go to Settings → Google → Ads and enable "Opt out of Ads Personalisation"
  • In-app: Access ad preferences via your account settings to manage consent
  • Premium subscription: Upgrading to Premium removes all advertising from your experience entirely

Analytics

We use analytics tools to understand how users engage with the app. Analytics data is aggregated and used only to improve the Service. You can opt out of analytics data collection in your account settings.

We comply with Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework and Google's advertising policies. We will always request your permission before tracking your activity across other apps and websites.

08

Data Security

We take the security of your personal data seriously and implement industry-standard technical and organisational measures to protect it, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit using TLS (Transport Layer Security)
  • Encryption of sensitive data at rest
  • Access controls ensuring only authorised personnel can access personal data
  • Regular security assessments and penetration testing
  • Incident response procedures for detecting and responding to data breaches
No system is 100% secure

While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure. In the event of a data breach that is likely to result in risk to your rights, we will notify you and the relevant supervisory authority as required by applicable law.

You are also responsible for keeping your account credentials secure. Do not share your password with anyone, and use a strong, unique password for your Skeep account.

09

Children's Privacy

Skeep is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

For users aged 13 to 15 in the EEA (or under the applicable age of digital consent in their country), we require verifiable parental or guardian consent before processing their personal data beyond what is strictly necessary to provide the Service. Certain features — including personalised advertising and some social features — are restricted for users under 16.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data without your consent, please contact us at hi@skeep.me and we will take prompt action to delete the data.
